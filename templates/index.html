Welcome to the SFD & BMD Plotter

This interactive project allows you to choose beam tyoe,input beam length, give concentrated(point) and distributed forces, and visualize Shear Force Diagrams (SFD) and Bending Moment Diagrams (BMD).

How it Works:
  • Choose the type of beam.
  • Enter the lengths of the beam sections.
  • Input concentrated(point) and distributed forces in the tables.
  • Submit to generate plots of SFD and BMD.
